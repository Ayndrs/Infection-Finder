Influenza

A viral infection that has been around for a while but still persists is influenza, more commonly known as the flu. You may know that every year, a new vaccine is offered for the flu, which tries to predict how the flu will mutate. The symptoms can vary greatly depending on your immune system's strengt, vaccination status, and other underlying health conditions. The symptoms are very similar to that of covid, so if you experience any of these, it is still highly encouraged to get tested for covid. Below is a list of symptoms for the flu.

  • 1 Fever or Chills
  • 2 Fatigue
  • 3 Cough
  • 4 Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing
  • 5 Sore throat
  • 6 Runny or stuffy nose
  • 7 Headache
