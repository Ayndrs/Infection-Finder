COVID

The Covid pandemic, which started back in 2019 has been continuing to spread globally, and it can be hard to tell if you have covid, due to the different strains and other viral infections such as the flu. The most recent variant as of today is the Omicron variant, which has been spreading like wildfire across the United States, and below is a list of symptoms that are correlated with the omicron variant. If you have 2 or more of these symptoms, it may be a good idea to get tested, and below will be a link to find covid testing sites near you.

  • 1 Fever or Chills
  • 2 Fatigue
  • 3 Cough
  • 4 Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing
  • 5 Loss of smell and / or taste
  • 6 Muscle pain / Body aches
